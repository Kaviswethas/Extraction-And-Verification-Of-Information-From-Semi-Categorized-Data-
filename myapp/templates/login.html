RAC

The Recruitment and Assessment Centre (RAC) is a corporate body under the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), part of the Ministry of Defence, Government of India.

RAC is responsible for recruitment, assessment, and training of scientists for DRDO and its allied agencies, such as the Aeronautical Development Agency (ADA). RAC manages selection processes for middle and senior-grade scientists.
